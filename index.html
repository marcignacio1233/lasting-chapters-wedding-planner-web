How it works

From first message to last dance

01

Enquiry & date check

Tell us your date and vision. We confirm availability within 48 hours.

02

Discovery call

A 30-minute conversation about your priorities, guest list, and budget — no obligation.

03

Proposal & contract

You receive a package tailored to your day, with clear inclusions and pricing.

04

Planning in motion

Vendor booking, styling decisions, and monthly check-ins, at a pace that suits you.

05
